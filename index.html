Shudong Hao (or, Mr.Holiday)

A passionate learner, and an artistic researcher

I am currently studying Computer Science in Beijing Forestry University. The main interests are Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing. I entitle myself "A passionate learner and an artistic researcher". I'm willing to learn everything that attaches my interests. Training rigorously in piano and painting for almost 15 years, I wish to view everything I love -- mathematics, computers and so forth -- in a more artistic way. So if I need an informal title, it would be "Artistic Researcher".

In August 27th, 2013, I debuted on the conference "International Conference on Document Analysis and Recognition 2013" in Washington DC, US, by presenting my paper Automated Error Detection and Correction of Chinese Characters in Written Essays Based on Weighted Finite-State Transducer. After that, several publications have been issued which can be downloaded in the "publications" section.

For a more comprehensive knowledge about me, please refer to the "profile" section.
